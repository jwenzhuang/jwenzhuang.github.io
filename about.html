About


I'm an artist, and freelance writer & editor. I recieved my BFA in Film/Photography from RISD and MA in American Studies from Brown. My interests are in critical pedagogy, media, and aesthetics, especially how labor and individual autonomy, how play and restraint, are, individually and simultaneously, enacted and denied within these structures. I've worked mainly in publishing and cultural non-profits.

For fun, I try and collect things on my Are.na. And watch a lot of movies on my blog, Code Unknown. There's also the newsletter I run, A Fountain.
