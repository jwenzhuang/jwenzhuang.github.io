About


I'm an artist and freelance writer and editor. I recieved my MA in American Studies from Brown University and my BFA in Film & Photography from Rhode Island School of Design. My research areas include critical pedagogy studies, critical university studies, media and aesthetics, especially how labor and individual autonomy are both enacted and denied in these structures. I've worked mainly in publishing and cultural non-profits.

For fun, I collect things on my Are.na. There's also my Twitter, Instagram, and the newsletter I run, A Fountain. For professional inquiries, you can email for my CV and for freelance work in copyediting, editing, proofreading, fact-checking, and writing.
